Our team 我們的團隊

創辦人暨首席室內設計師/Lead Designer, Eric Chen


川禾室內設計有限公司為一綜合型空間設計公司。由國內外學經歷豐富之景觀、建築及室內設計師組成，為多向度思考及專案整合之設計團隊。在室內設計的專業中，我們勇於嘗試，致力於生活家的想像。在人本的生活環境中，我們尊重使用者的需求，結合客戶之概念與想像，讓空間設計衍生出更多的可能性。

我們相信，唯有不斷的溝通與討論，才能達成完美的平衡。

We are a specialized team that consists of professionals majored in Interior, Architecture, Landscape, and Arts worldwide with comprehensive designs. In design profession, we integrate diverse thoughts into creationary spaces, challenge conceivable approaches to meet needs for our clients, and proceed proposals with abundant sensitivities, imaginations and concepts as value as foremost for living.Therefore, our primary consideration and goal in developing possibilities of spaces is to achieve perfection in balance through ongoing beliefs, and bilateral communications.
